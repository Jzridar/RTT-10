Breakfast smoothie

Ingredients

  • Almond
  • Pista
  • Walnut
  • Dates
  • Raisin
  • Cranberry
  • Prunes

About

This breakfast recipe is an amazing all in one nutritious smoothie that gives you all day energy,simple to make yet delicious in taste.

Method

  1. Soak all the nuts in a bowl,all the dry fruits in a seperate bowl
  2. Overnight soaking preferred
  3. Next day morning remove the skin from almond and wash the nuts in good water
  4. Add them to the blender with soaked fruits and its water
  5. Blend until smooth for drinking consistancy
  6. Pour it on a glass and serve it with nuts toppings
